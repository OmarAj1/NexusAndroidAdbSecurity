# Privacy Policy for Nexus Security

**Last updated:** December 23, 2025

Nexus Security ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app uses your device's data.

**1. Information We Collect**
We may access the following information on your device:
* **Phone State (READ_PHONE_STATE):** This permission is used solely to access unique device identifiers (such as ADB Serial Number) required to establish a secure Wireless Debugging connection.
* **Network Information:** Used to discover Wireless Debugging ports on your local network.

**2. How We Use Your Information**
* To establish a local ADB connection to your own device.
* To facilitate the removal of bloatware and system management tasks initiated by you.
* **We do not transmit your personal data or device identifiers to external servers.** All operations are performed locally on your device.
